History

10 Years Farm Traditions

Since

2011

Our industry has its stages vertically integrated for greater efficiency and higher product quality. The sonali chicken production process begins with the grandparent breeder flocks.
The breeder flocks are raised to maturity in grandparent growing and laying farms where fertile eggs are produced. Pullets hatch from the fertile eggs, and they are sent to the hatcheries.
Shortly after 21 days, when the eggs hatch, the day-old chicks are sold to customers owning broiler farms.

Since

2021

There, the farmers care for and raise the chicks according to our company’s standards, guidelines and process for a better growth of the chicks. When the broilers reach the desired processing weight within a time frame of approximately 60 days, they are caught and taken to processing plants.
The finished chicken products are sent to distribution centres then transported to customers who sell the chicken to consumers. We also assist our customers in selling the finished products.
